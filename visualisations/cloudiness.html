City Latitude vs Cloudiness



There was no observable correlation between city latitude and cloudiness. Of interest in this data is the distibution of cloudiness across the selected cities. There is a high number of 0% and 100% readings. The cluster of results of 75%, 40% and 20% are most likely due to rounding in the data collection process.
